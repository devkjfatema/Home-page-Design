Welcome to Meadowbrook Dental
Laser Clinic in Chilliwack

Meadowbrook Dental Laser Clinic provides leading-edge laser dentistry to residents of the Fraser Valley. Located in Chilliwack, Meadowbrook Dental specializes in the use of lasers to treat a wide range of dental problems.

Laser dentistry is a safe and effective alternative to the dental drill when fillings are needed. Lasers are used to remove decay and prepare teeth for fillings painlessly. Depending on the size of the cavity, lasers reduce or eliminate the need for freezing.

Owned by Dr. Don Utas since 1995 and specializing in laser dentistry since 2006, Meadowbrook Dental Laser Clinic is a unique practice in the area. Patients come from all over the Fraser Valley.

Our Dentist

Meet Dr. Don Utas

Mastership Certification
World Clinical Laser Institute
Since 1995
Serving Chilliwack

Dr. Don Utas received his Dental degree from the University of Saskatchewan in 1994 and bought Meadowbrook Dental in Chilliwack a year later. As a lifelong learner, he has earned a Diploma of Technology at BCIT and a Bachelor of Education at Simon Fraser University and worked as a Park Ranger and high school teacher before devoting his life to helping people through dentistry.

Since 2006, Dr. Utas has been committed to offering the most up-to-date technology to his patients through laser dentistry. He received Mastership Certification in Laser Dentistry at the World Clinical Laser Institute in California, and he continues to learn about new procedures through continuing education in the US and Canada.

“I have fun with what I do. Lasers allow me to do more for my patients and, in my opinion, are a better way than using the old-fashioned methods.”
— Dr. Don Utas
